Style that sustains the planet

EcoTrend is a student startup demo — a simple marketplace to buy, sell, and exchange pre-loved or upcycled clothes. Build your greener wardrobe and help reduce textile waste.

Team of 3 — Business, Tech, Marketing. Demo uses local browser storage. For a production backend use Firebase or a simple Node API.
How it works
  1. Browse & search sustainable clothing
  2. List your item to sell or offer an exchange
  3. Contact seller & arrange pickup/shipping
Tip: Use the Sell form to add a product — images are saved to your browser.

About EcoTrend

EcoTrend is a student-built demo marketplace to showcase an MVP for sustainable fashion. It focuses on circular fashion: resale, upcycling, and education. Use this as your entrepreneurship project to show product, business model, and demo website.

Business Model

  • Commission on successful sales (demo: not implemented)
  • Premium listings for verified eco-brands
  • Workshops & brand partnerships
Quick metrics (demo)
- Target audience: 18–30 urban students and young professionals
- Customer acquisition: campus ambassadors and Instagram
- Cost structure: minimal (hosting + marketing)
